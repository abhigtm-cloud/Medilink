# 📱 MediLink - Healthcare Booking App
## SIMPLE VIRAL CAPTION

---

Tired of calling 10 hospitals just to book a doctor's appointment? 

I was too. So I built **MediLink** 🏥

**What it does:**
→ Find a hospital
→ Pick a doctor  
→ Book an appointment
→ Done ✅

That's it. Takes 60 seconds. No calls. No waiting on hold. No confusion.

**Why it matters:**
Millions of patients waste hours every month trying to book appointments. Meanwhile, hospitals struggle to manage bookings and doctors show up to empty slots.

**What we fixed:**
✅ Instant hospital & doctor search
✅ Real-time availability (no double bookings)
✅ Emergency mode - find nearest hospital in seconds
✅ Done. Live now on Android.

If you've ever struggled to book a doctor's appointment, share this. If you're building in healthcare, tech, or startups - let's connect.
